Choosy Help: Uninstalling Choosy

To remove Choosy from your system, follow these steps:

  • Open System Preference, and click General.
  • Change the Default web browser setting to something other than Choosy, e.g. Safari or Google Chrome.
  • Find `Choosy.app` in your Applications folder, and drag it to the trash.
