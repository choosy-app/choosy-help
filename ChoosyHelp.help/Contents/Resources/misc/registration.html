Choosy Help: Trials & registration

Choosy is free to use without any limitations for 45 days. While you are in your trial period, a reminder will be displayed at the bottom of the settings window:

If you find Choosy useful and want to continue using it beyond the trial period, you must purchase a licence from the Choosy web site.

You are free to use the same licence key on all the Macs you use (your MacBook and your iMac, your home computer and your work computer, etc.).
